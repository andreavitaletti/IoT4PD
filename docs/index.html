Introduction

Smart Object

An object becomes smart when thanks to the technology it can do something better than its non-smart original version or it can even do something completely new and appreciated by the users.

From smart Objects to Internet of Things

A smart object is part of the Internet of Thing (IoT) if it is connected and thanks to its connectivity, it is part of an ecosystem that allows to do something better than its non-connected original version or it can even do something completely new and appreciated by the members of the ecosystem.

IoT is more than remote control

Collective Intelligence

Together we can do more than individually Collective intelligence is shared or group intelligence that emerges from the collaboration, collective efforts, and competition of many individuals and appears in consensus decision making.

The Evolutio of Internet

Maturity Level

  1. Old fashion mechanic car
  2. A modern car with sensors and actuators
  3. The car is connected and deliver data on its usage (e.g. path, levels, usage etc.)
  4. The car can be remotely controlled
  5. on-car algorithms (e.g. ABS) and off-car algorithms (e.g. prediction of maintenance) to optimize performance
  6. Self-Driving car
  7. Car-2-Car
  8. Car-2-Car-2-Gas-2-Traffic Light ...
